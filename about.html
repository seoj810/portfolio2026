About

A little about me.

I'm a product designer based in New York City with a background in visual design. Craft and attention to detail are core to how I work.

Early in my career I worked at agencies, which gave me exposure to a wide range of design work across platforms and mediums — from marketing and branding to pitch decks and mobile apps. That breadth built a strong foundation and made me flexible in ways that have served me well as a product designer.

I've always been drawn to new and emerging technology, and my career has naturally reflected that. I've worked across VR, AR, and AI, on both software and hardware. I'm currently at Meta working on AI wearable products. Working in emerging tech means getting comfortable with ambiguity, and it's an environment I've learned to thrive in.

Open to new work — looking for my next thing.
